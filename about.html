About Laufey

Laufey (pronounced LAY-vee) is an Icelandic-Chinese singer and musician. She is known for making soft, relaxing music that mixes jazz and pop. She grew up playing classical music and later began sharing songs online, where people quickly fell in love with her warm voice and calm, cozy style.

Laufey writes about love, growing up, and everyday feelings in a simple and heartfelt way. Her songs are peaceful and comforting, and many listeners say her music helps them feel calm. Today, she performs around the world and continues to inspire young people to enjoy jazz and express themselves through music.
